About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at Ottawa University with a growing passion for UX/UI design and front-end development. I believe great digital experiences begin with thoughtful, user-centered design.

While completing my degree, I've worked on several small web design projects that helped me develop a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and responsive layouts using Bootstrap. I'm currently learning JavaScript and experimenting with modern frameworks to expand my skills.

My approach to design is methodical yet creative — I like to sketch out ideas, build interactive prototypes, and iterate based on feedback. I'm particularly interested in accessible design, minimalist interfaces, and how typography and color theory shape user experience.
